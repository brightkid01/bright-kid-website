Benefits of Early Childhood Education with Us!

Over 85% of a child’s brain development occurs prior to age of 6.

Activity-based Pedagogy and Enriched Engagement

90% of a child’s brain development happens before the age of 5. (source: Harvard Centre of Developing Child). Children have a natural desire to learn. Dr. Maria Montessori recognized that children have an intrinsic motivation to learn even and engage in games and activities, children learn to grasp an object, they learn to stand by trying again and again and they master walking. Physical development activities not only make the concepts more tangible but also appeal to children's diverse learning styles and take advantage of their multi-sensory strengths. If children are physically involved in doing activities, they are more apt to be mentally engaged.

Entire Teaching Content, Resources are just a Scan away with Anytime, Anywhere Teacher

Parents get a Virtual-Teacher on the pages of the books and worksheets that we provide in our book kit. My Bright books are unique and are Augmented Reality (AR) enabled which delivers rich interactive content online focused on curriculum-based education. With images, alphabets, words, rhymes, stories being precisely projected and narrated just like having a real-teacher do the teaching, early childhood learning and concept building are strengthened and accelerated in our preschool programs. We are the first in the world to innovate to add this technology feature to Montessori and Preschool Learning materials that we make.

World Class Hands-on Learning & Montessori Materials in our Kits

Hands-on learning & creative play are important ingredients for learning. Dr. Maria Montessori felt strongly that the hand and brain must develop in harmony. Our hands-on Montessori materials are well crafted mostly by using natural materials like wood and boards. These are categorized into few activity types such as: up to Playgroup (age up to 3 years) - Eye-hand coordination, Music and Movement, Practical Life, Arts and Crafts and Language. For Children of age group 3 to 6 years- Exercises of Practical life, sensorial, Language, Arithmetic, and Enrichment. Children in preschools and at home love not only exploring but also repeating the activities. Montessori and other materials that we would provide are made of natural materials like wood and boards which are long-lasting for much-repeated use.

Technology at its best to Track Progress of Child

Absorbent minds of children need the right nourishment at the right time with age-curated online learning resources for preschoolers. Learning resources are made available based on the child's age. With resources set up in the most clutter-free form, Children learn from beginning skill level through kindergarten proficiency with hundreds of lessons. A guided step-by-step approach to learning that progresses sequentially. As children advance through the curriculum, lessons, Activities become incrementally challenging in easy steps that suit the child. Parents can choose to log in anytime as well and see progress or even choose to help the child with the material.
